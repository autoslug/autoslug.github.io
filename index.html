WELCOME TO AUTOSLUG
Autoslug is an organization affiliated with the Baskin School of Engineering that specializes in implementing AI, machine learning, and computer vision in practical applications. Our goal is to provide students with opportunities to gain hands-on experience and move beyond the theoretical aspects of these advanced concepts in order to apply them. This would lead to delving into their intersections with humans, data, and various hardware environments.
